Pimp my chordsheet!

Simply edit the song in the left window, and a beautiful, print ready PDF version will instantly be shown on the right, complete with great looking chord diagrams.

About

I created this site because I was sick of printing out ugly looking ASCII songs from tablature sites. I wanted real chord diagrams, non monospace fonts, and a column layout to easily get a song with all verses on one page. This site is my attempt to make it as easy as possible to get a beautiful printed version from any of the millions of songs you can find out there on the internet.
